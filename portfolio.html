BASIC WEB

Basic, you say? Despite calling it "basic", it was an intensive introduction to the primary tools of Multimedia Design. Human brains are hardwired to connect the dots and make sense out of everything the eyes see. In Basic Web I learned about different layout principles so we can dominate better the usage of positive space (represented by the elements we use) and negative space (the background). I was also introduced to a beautiful language: coding!


01.02.01

Basic mobile website

How we arrange the elements in the negative space we are given is crucial in order to create something that the brain can recognize easily.

The Gestalt Laws can be compared to the Ten Commendaments of the Design Bible. During this project I used the law of symmetry in the three column layout (for desktop) and single column layout (for mobile). I have also used the law of proximity in the navigation menu.

01.03.01.

Given Style XD Prototype

We were given a style to work with during our first theme. Mine was Deconstructivism, a movement of postmodern architecture which appeared in the 1980s. After gathering information I proceeded to curating wireframes and moodboards for this given style. The result is a XD prototype for an imaginary (mobile) website that promotes the history and artists during this genre's period.

01.04.01.

Responsive website

During Basic Mobile Website I discovered that html is like a canvas and css is our brush and paint. During Responsive Website I learned that not only I can style the content of my html but also make it suitable for every device and every screen size, no matter how large or small! Hello CSS Grids and media queries!

A Story Without Words

During Basic Content out first assignement was to create a video without using words or dialogue, that was filmed with a smartphone and did not exceed 2 minutes.

03.02.02

Storyboard

In our storyboard the main character (Lisa) breaks the 4th wall and speaks directly to the viewer. Apart from this obvious dramaturgical element, gestures can be detected during the point-of-no-return, when our main character meets a man that seems interested in her. The romantic story in our video makes the mood of the movie very light and funny. We took this approach because we wanted to keep the viewer entertained while learning this basic rules of cycling.
